Options
  • Show Mailbox Banner?
    Banner alerts you of items in mailbox
  • Show Comm Center Banner?
    Banner alerts you of Community Center progress
  • Auto-Refresh Mailboxes?
    Refreshes when items change in Mailboxes
  • Show Loading Spinner?
    Show loading indicator when you change menus
  • Show Chat?
    Show the right-side Chat Panel
  • Show Online Status?
    Other players can tell if you are Online
  • Auto-close Alerts?
    Alerts close after a few seconds
  • Harvest Ready Notifications?
    Notification when crops are ready to harvest
    Currently iOS Only (IN BETA)
  • Hide Help Requests?
    Enables option to hide requests
  • Dark Mode UI?
    Dark theme for the Game
  • Show Profile Stats?
    Show Item Totals and more on your Profile
  • Lock Tracked Items?
    Locks items when tracking a help request
  • Background Music
    Currently only works on iOS app
  • Text Size
    Adjust the size of fonts larger/smaller
  • Pet Whistle Minimum
    Number of items ready before banner appears
  • Launcher Amount
    Number of Large Nets to launch per use
    Set between 10 and 50 and watch your Inventory Cap
  • Safe Mode
    Disables community features and gold buying

    With Safe Mode ON, your account will not be able to buy Gold, send/receive Messages or view the in-game Chat panel.
